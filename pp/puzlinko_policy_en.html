Privacy Policy for Puzlinko

Last Updated: May 2, 2024

Thank you for playing Puzlinko! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our game.

1. Information Collection

Puzlinko accesses the following through Apple Game Center:

  • Leaderboard Scores: When you choose to submit scores, they are stored only on Apple's Game Center servers.
  • Game Center ID: Your anonymous Game Center player identifier for score attribution.

We do not store any leaderboard data on our own servers.

2. How Information is Used

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Game Center Leaderboards: Displaying player rankings through Apple's Game Center service.
  • Local Gameplay: Saving puzzle progress only on your device.

3. Data Sharing

All leaderboard functionality is provided by Apple Game Center:

  • Score submission is optional and requires your consent.
  • We do not access or store your Game Center data.

4. Your Controls

You manage leaderboard participation through:

  • Initial Consent: We ask permission before submitting any scores to Game Center.

5. Data Security

All leaderboard data is secured by:

  • Apple's Game Center infrastructure
  • Your device's local game data is never transmitted

6. Policy Changes

Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
